News Interactives is a tool and pattern library for interactive stories. Interactive stories, also known as "interactives" or "news apps", are stories that assembles data, visualisation, multimedia into a coherent narrative and which presence can be altered by user interaction.

For who is News Interactives?

This tool is here to help and excite journalists to tell their stories in another way. It can be used at news organisations that have an interactive team or team of developers. But "News Interactives" is here for newsrooms without any technical support aswell, providing inspiration and tools that can help you to make a certain interactive.

Thesis

This tool is part of my thesis "New ways of telling stories in journalism". It's a study on the effects of interactive storytelling and how the process of telling stories will change when making interactives.

My Story

One example of why interactive storytelling is important and effective, is the story of this product itself. I built this tool during my graduation at The Guardian. I wanted to work there so bad, so I decided to make an interactive of myself. It proved useful.

Made possible by The Guardian
