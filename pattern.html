Pattern

Use A Timeline

Ways to visualise data

A linear display of events, based on time. A good way to give an overview of events, embedded with multimedis.

Use When

  • You have data based on time
  • Your story is too big/diverse
  • The story moves through time and space

There are 4 interactives that use this pattern

The state of our union is... dumber

Guardian US

An interactive data visualisation about how the linguistic standard of the presidential address has declined

Snowfall

NY Times

An immersive linear interactive about an avalanche at Tunnel Creek. Won a Pullitzer.
